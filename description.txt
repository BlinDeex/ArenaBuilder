Customizable Arena adds 2 items and 1 tile to craft instant arena builder, so far you can set arena width,
height, floors, will it clear area or not(normally used for underground arenas), theme, campfires and/or heart lanterns placement.

to start you will need 20 wood for broken arena module and 50 stone blocks with 4 iron ingots for arena customizer, last item is obtainable only through arena customizer with stats you decide on.
